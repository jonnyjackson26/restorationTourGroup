Zoom meeting to prepare for tour

Day One - Tuesday October 8th

Arrive (plane tickets not included in pricing) arrive by 8:00 pm send travel arrangements to restoration tours as soon as you have them (minimum of 30 days prior to travel) if arriving prior to 8:00 transport will meet you at Cleveland Hopkins Airport and transport you to hotel (transport and hotel costs included in pricing)

8:30 Welcome meeting at hotel

Day Two - Wednesday October 9th

Today we will load the motor coach and off we go! We will begin with a grand overview of Kirtland. We will answer these questions (and many more!) What brought the prophet to Kirtland? Why Kirtland? And how did the Lord prepare the area for the restoration?

  • Isaac & Lucy Morley farm
  • Newel K Whitney Store
  • School of the Prophets
  • Whitney family lived
  • Recreated sawmill and Ashery
  • Joseph & Emma Smith home
  • Fairport Harbor
  • Painesville

Breakfast and lunch are included today, dinner is not included in our pricing.

Day Three - Thursday October 10th

On Thursday will visit the site of the Orange conference and learn about the witnesses to the Book of Mormon. We will learn about the Saints who came to Kirtland from Orange. For lunch we will visit Cleveland’s famous Little Italy for an incredible lunch and dessert! After lunch we will take a quick break from church history and visit one of the most spectacular cemeteries in the United States. We will visit the tomb of a president, inventors, and a Rockefeller!

  • Spaulding manuscript
  • Parley Pratt and the Stu-boy chase

Breakfast is included today, but lunch and dinner will be on your own.

Day Four - Friday October 11th

We have a GREAT day today! We will travel to the Community of Christ chapel in Kirtland where we will be taught by the local Community of Christ pastor Nancy Cervi about their beliefs, and their experiences as being stewards of the Kirtland Temple for nearly 144 years. Next, we will go and learn about Sidney Rigdon in one of his church buildings. On our way to the Johnson Farm we will see the home that Lorenzo Snow was born in and make a quick stop at a cemetery to hear a story about a member who left the church but couldn’t leave the church alone. We will have an in-depth discussion on ‘The Vision’ and the impact that section 76 had on the world. Near the end of the day, we will turn our attention to the temple, and we will learn about the Endowment of power and temple ordinances that occurred in the Kirtland Temple. At the end of our day, we will visit an Amish home and have an incredible home-cooked meal with AMAZING pies! While we visit we will see how the Amish live, and will be able to explore their property.

  • Community of Christ chapel
  • Johnson Farm
  • Saw mill
  • Amish home

All meals are included today.

Day Five - Saturday October 12th

As we wind down our tour, we will save the best for last! We will tour the Kirtland Temple, we will learn about some faithful saints who are buried in the Kirtland Temple cemetery and then we will learn about the prophecies that were made about Kirtland post 1838, including the transfer of stewardship of the Kirtland Temple in 2024. We will conclude our tour with a great lunch.

  • Kirtland Temple
  • Kirtland Temple cemetery

Breakfast and lunch are included today.
